Our Services

Game Library

Has a collection of tabletop games that students can borrow for personal use or group activities.

Game Rentals

Allow students to rent tabletop games for a specific duration.

Tournaments

Host tabletop game tournaments or competitions within the school. Recognize and reward winners with certificates or small prizes.

Study Breaks

Organize study breaks during exam periods where students can relax and unwind with tabletop games.

Game Design Workshops

Arrange workshops on basic game design principles. Encourage students to express their creativity through designing and prototyping games

Online Gaming Community

Established an online platform for students to connect, discuss games, and schedule virtual gaming sessions.

Top Games Top tier games available on meeples

UNO

UNO is a multi-player card game in which the objective is to be the first player to get rid of all the cards in their hand. Each player is dealt 7 cards and players take turn drawing cards from the deck.

Explore Games

WEREWOLF

One Night Ultimate Werewolf is a fast-paced game for 3-10 players where everyone is dealt a secret role. Many of these roles have special abilities that will aid that player in gaining information. In the course of only one night and the following morning, the players will determine who among them is a werewolf

Explore Games

CATAN

Catan has a few different things in it, but mostly it's a resource management game, with aspects of strategy. You have to plan where you're going to build settlements, and depending on where you build you have a chance (by rolling dice) of getting resources, which you then use to build roads and settlements.

Explore Games

COUP

Coup is a social deduction card game designed by Rikki Tahta and published in 2012 by Indie Boards & Cards and La Mame Games. Players are given two cards and attempt to eliminate the other players by lying and calling their bluffs until only one player remains.
